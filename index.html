About Elizade University

It is with pleasure that I welcome you to Elizade University, a citadel of knowledge that seeks to be a globally competitive institution, which produces entrepreneurial, innovative and ethical graduates. The University is driven by strategic developmental agenda, which is embedded in the Vision of the Founder, Chief Michael Ade.Ojo. These include engagement of quality faculty members to highly complement quality students, leveraging on the infrastructural endowment of the University for excellence, innovativeness and creativity, zealousness in academic delivery and research, attainment of world-class status and primal position in Nigeria, deployment of modern technological tools in teaching, research and administration and entrepreneurial empowermentthrough robust skill acquisition programmes. This carefully drafted developmental agenda has continued to drive the University towards the attainment of its Vision, as evident in the various feats recorded by the University in the recent time.
